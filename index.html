Commission

Design Project

My projects typically include logo creation, poster design, and visual branding. Depending on the complexity and scope of the project, the pricing ranges from IDR 200,000 to IDR 2,000,000. For international clients, the starting price is around $50 per project. Each project is tailored to meet the specific needs of the client, with a focus on delivering clear, impactful, and visually consistent results. If you're interested, feel free to contact me directly.

Illustration Project

My illustration work is created in my own unique and creative art style, and I only accept commissions that follow this personal style. Pricing starts from $150 per project for full commissions. For individual elements, the rates are as follows:

  • Background illustration starts from IDR 50,000
  • Creature illustration (head only) starts from IDR 75,000
  • Half-body illustration starts from IDR 125,000
  • Full-body illustration starts from IDR 250,000 (including a simple background)

More detailed or customized backgrounds and complex scenes are available upon request. Feel free to contact me for specific needs or to discuss special project ideas.

Photography/Videography

I offer photography and videography services for various needs, such as promotional content and event documentation. Pricing starts from IDR 150,000 per session, depending on factors such as project type, session duration, and travel distance to the location. Rates are flexible and can be adjusted based on the specific requirements of each project. If you're interested, feel free to contact me directly.
